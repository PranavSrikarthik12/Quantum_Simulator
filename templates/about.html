About This Project

Hello! I'm Sarvesh Ganesan, a 3rd year CSE student at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Chennai Campus.

This Quantum Simulator is part of my passion for making quantum computing more interactive, visual, and accessible. Whether you're exploring Bell states or constructing a QFT from scratch, this tool is designed to help you understand quantum principles with real-time simulation.

The project is built with Flask, Qiskit, and Bootstrap 5, offering both functionality and flair. You can simulate different quantum algorithms, generate Bloch sphere visuals, and dive into gate-level customization.
